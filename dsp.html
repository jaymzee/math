Sampled Signal

$$ \hat{x}(t) = \sum_{-\infty}^{\infty} x(nT) \delta(t - nT) $$

Discrete-Time Fourier Transform

$$\begin{align} \hat{X}(\Omega) &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \hat{x}(t) e^{-j \Omega t} dt \\ &= \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \left[ \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x(nT) \delta(t - nT) \right] e^{-j \Omega t} dt \\ &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x(nT) e^{-j \Omega nT} \end{align}$$

$$ \omega = 2 \pi \frac{f}{f_s} = \frac{\Omega}{f_s} $$

$$\begin{align} \hat{X}(\omega) &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x(nT) e^{-j \omega n} \\ &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x[n] e^{-j \omega n} \quad \text{alternative notation} \\ &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x_n e^{-j \omega n} \end{align}$$

Z Transform

$$\begin{align} \hat{X}(z) &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x(nT) z^{-n} \\ &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x[n] z^{-n} \quad \text{alternative notation} \\ &= \sum_{n = 0}^{\infty} x_n z^{-n} \end{align}$$

Transfer Function

$$ Y(z) = \frac{ b_0 + b_1 z^{-1} + b_2 z^{-2} } { 1 + a_1 z^{-1} + a_2 z^{-2} } X(z) $$

Difference Equation

$$\begin{align} y_n + a_1 y_{n-1} + a_2 y_{n-2} &= b_0 + b_1 x_{n-1} + b_2 x_{n-2} \\ y_n &= b_0 + b_1 x_{n-1} + b_2 x_{n-2} - a_1 y_{n-1} - a_2 y_{n-2} \end{align}$$
