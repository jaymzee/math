chain rule

given `f(x,y,z)` where `x`, `y`, and `z` are functions `x(t)`,`y(t)`, and `z(t)` respectively:
`d/dt f(x,y) = (del f(x,y,z))/(del x) dx/dt + (del f(x,y,z))/(del y) dy/dt + (del f(x,y,z))/(del z) dz/dt`

$$ \frac{d}{dt} f(x,y) = \frac{\partial f(x,y,z)}{\partial x} \frac{dx}{dt} + \frac{\partial f(x,y,z)}{\partial y} \frac{dy}{dt} + \frac{\partial f(x,y,z)}{\partial z} \frac{dz}{dt} $$
