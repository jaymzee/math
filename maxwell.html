Gradient, Divergence, and Curl in cartesian coordinates

`grad phi(x,y,z) = hati (delphi)/(delx) + hatj (delphi)/(dely) + hatk (delphi)/(delz)`
`grad * bbA = (delA_x)/(delx) + (delA_y)/(dely) + (delA_z)/(delz)`
`grad xx bbA = |[ hati , hatj , hatk ], [ del/(delx) , del/(dely) , del/(delz) ], [ A_x , A_y , A_z ]|`

Gradient, Divergence, and Curl generalized to curvilinear coordinates

`grad u(x_1,x_2,x_3) = sum_(i=1)^3 hate_i 1/h_i (delu)/(delx_i)`
`grad * bbA = 1/(h_1 h_2 h_3) [(del(h_2 h_3 A_1))/(delx_1) + (del(h_1 h_3 A_2))/(delx_2) + (del(h_1 h_2 A_3))/(delx_3)]`
`grad xx bbA = 1/(h_1 h_2 h_3) |[ hate_1 h_1 , hate_2 h_2 , hate_3 h_3 ], [ del/(delx_1) , del/(delx_2) , del/(delx_3) ], [ h_1 A_1 , h_2 A_2 , h_3 A_3 ]|`
in cartesian coordinates `x_1 = x`, `x_2 = y`, `x_3 = z` and `h_1 = h_2 = h_3 = 1` and `hate_1 = hati`, `hate_2 = hatj`, `hate_3 = hatk`
in cylindrical coordinates `x_1 = r`, `x_2 = phi`, `x_3 = z` and `h_1 = 1`, `h_2 = r`, `h_3 = 1` and `hate_1 = bba_r`, `hate_2 = bba_phi`, `hate_3 = bba_z`
in spherical coordinates `x_1 = R`, `x_2 = theta`, `x_3 = phi` and `h_1 = 1`, `h_2 = R`, `h_3 = R sin theta` and `hate_1 = bba_R`, `hate_2 = bba_theta`, `hate_3 = bba_phi`

Electrodynamics

`grad * bbD = ρ_V`
`grad * bbB = 0`
`grad xx bbE = -(delbbB)/(delt)`
`grad xx bbH = (delbbD)/(delt) + bbJ`

`bbE = -grad bbV`
`bbD = ε_0 bbE + bbP`
`bbD = ε_0 bbE + chi_e ε_0 bbE = ε_0 ε_r bbE = ε bbE`

`bbB = mu_0(bbH + bbM)`
`bbB = mu_0 bbH + mu_0 chi_m bbH = mu_0 mu_r bbH = mu bbH`

`bbF = q(bbE + bbv xx bbB)`
